Cheesecake

This is a delicious cheesecake recipe that you'll love. It's creamy, rich, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract

Steps to make cheesecake

Here's how to make it:

  1. Prepare the crust.
  2. Mix the filling ingredients until smooth.
  3. Pour the filling over the crust.
  4. Bake until set.
  5. Chill before serving.
