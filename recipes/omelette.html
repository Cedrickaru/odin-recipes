Omelette

This is a classic omelette recipe that's perfect for breakfast, brunch, or any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fillings of your choice (e.g., cheese, vegetables, ham)

Steps to make omelette

Here's how to make it:

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a skillet.
  3. Pour egg mixture into the skillet.
  4. Add fillings to one half of the omelette.
  5. Flip the other half over the fillings.
  6. Cook until set and golden brown.
